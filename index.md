\# Privacy Policy — PC Usage Timer

\*\*Last Updated: 1st of December 2025\*\*



\## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how \*\*PC Usage Timer\*\* (“the App”) processes information on your device.  

The App operates entirely offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the developer or to third parties.



By using the App, you acknowledge and agree to the practices outlined in this policy.



---



\## 2. Information the App Processes

The App processes certain device usage information \*\*solely on your local machine\*\*.  

This information is used only for providing the App’s functionality.



The types of data processed locally include:



\- Application usage duration  

\- Total time spent in active sessions  

\- Timer history and usage statistics  



The App does \*\*not\*\* collect personal identifiers or sensitive information.



---



\## 3. Data Storage and Security

All data generated or processed by the App is stored \*\*locally\*\* on your device within the App’s data directories.  

The App does not transmit data externally or communicate with any servers.



Any locally stored data remains under your control and may be deleted by uninstalling the App or removing its associated files.



---



\## 4. Data Sharing and Transmission

The App does \*\*not\*\*:



\- Transmit data to the developer  

\- Share data with third parties  

\- Use analytics platforms  

\- Employ advertising networks  

\- Connect to the internet for data collection  



Your usage data remains private and isolated on your device.



---



\## 5. Permissions

The App may request certain system permissions required for its intended functionality, including:



\- Background activity access  

\- Access to application usage metrics  



These permissions are used exclusively for enabling the App’s features and are never used for data transmission or sharing.



---



\## 6. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal data and is safe for users of all ages.  

However, it is not specifically targeted toward children.
